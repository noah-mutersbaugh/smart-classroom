Before You Visit

For the continued safety of all participants, we ask that you adhere to the following expectations.

  • Complete the online self-assessment prior to arriving on campus/ at the Learning Center. Staff will ask to see your passed assessment at check-in. General public should use this self-assessment form. Please stay home if you don't feel well.
  • Visit the Lakers Together website for the most up to date information regarding campus face covering requirements and other safety protocol.s
  • If you or anyone in your party are under the age of 18, be sure to have our waiver completed by the parent/ legal guardian. Signatures by older siblings, relatives, or chaperones will not be accepted.
